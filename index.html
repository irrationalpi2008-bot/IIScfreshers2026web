The Onboarding Timeline

Your Freshers Journey Checklist

Step 01

Rank & Name Verification

Contact a representative below to send your approximate rank, name, and branch. This ensures a spam-free, secure community verified strictly for incoming undergraduates.

Step 02

Community WhatsApp Entry

Get added to the official IISc UG Freshers 2026 WhatsApp group. Meet your batchmates, start group chats, and read curated resource lists prepared by seniors.

Step 03

Hostel & Course Prep

Collaborate with seniors to complete your hostel allocation forms, medical check-up checklists, fee submissions, and course registration steps without hiccups.

Step 04

Campus Arrival & Meetups

Arrive at the beautiful, green green campus! Attend ice-breaker sessions, hostel meetups, and the official undergraduate orientation ceremony together with your new family.
